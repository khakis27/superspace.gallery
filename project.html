ABOUT.SUPERSPACE

INTRO

Superspace is an immersive metaverse dedicated to cultural and artistic expressions, serving as a dynamic venue for different exhibitions.

THE SQUARE

Superstudio's utopian vision of the future was based on the square. They called the black and white grid, created from repeating the square, a “supersurface” on which society could project new ways of building and living. Crafted with modular cubes, Superspace is a versatile and adaptable environment that easily transforms to accommodate various types of exhibitions, showcasing its fluid and adaptable design. the space is designed so that it can be used as a digital twin of real exhibition spaces.
