LINKED.CUBES

INTRO

Welcome to "Linked Cubes," an immersive exhibition that delves into the fascinating convergence of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Metaverses. This virtual space seamlessly connects multiple metaverses, each meticulously crafted as part of the Anthropology of Media course at Politecnico di Milano.

EXHIBITION

As we navigate through the intricacies of the digital realm, the exhibition serves as a captivating exploration of the symbiotic relationship between AI and metaverses. Each linked cube represents a distinct metaverse, showcasing the diverse interpretations and creative expressions born out of the Anthropology of Media program. Venture into these interconnected realms, where AI algorithms and virtual landscapes coalesce to redefine the boundaries of human experience. From the ethereal landscapes shaped by algorithms to the lifelike avatars embodying artificial intelligence, "Linked Cubes" offers a glimpse into the future of immersive digital environments. The exhibition not only highlights the technical prowess of AI but also underscores its impact on the cultural and social dimensions of metaverses. The curated metaverses are a testament to the innovative intersections between technology and anthropology, providing a unique lens through which to examine the evolving landscape of communication and connectivity. Join us in this visionary exploration, where the virtual and the artificial converge, blurring the lines between reality and the digital frontier. "Linked Cubes" invites you to contemplate the possibilities that arise when AI and metaverses intertwine, sparking new dialogues and shaping the future of interactive digital experiences.
